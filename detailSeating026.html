BODI User Guide
  • How can BODI be adjusted?

    BODI offers several points of adjustment across the entire chair, including:

    - Synchro-tilt mechanism, allowing for more intelligent reclining.

    - Tension adjustment, a knob on the underside of the chair that controls resistance to body weight.

    - Three-position locking mechanism, a mechanism that controls the tilt of the chair’s back with three different locking positions.

    - Four-way adjustable arms, which can expand to add 1” of arm width in four directions.

    - Seat depth adjustment, controlled by a lever beside the seat that allows you to slide the seat forwards or backwards as needed.

    - Lumbar support that can glide up or down the back of the seat to provide further back support.

    - Sliding seat that adapts to all frame sizes and sitting styles.

    - Height adjustment, easily adjustable via a lever underneath the seat, offering a height range of 17.5" - 23"

  • What is the Synchro-Tilt Mechanism?

    The Synchro-Tilt mechanism is a unique way of controlling seat position that combines a tension adjuster, three-position locking mechanism, an upright stop, and a tension dial into one complete system for setting the position your chair needs to rest at to keep you comfortable.

  • Does BODI offer lumbar support?

    BODI’s lumbar support is easily adjusted by placing your hands on the support on the back of the chair, and gliding it up or down into the desired position.

  • How can you remove BODI’s seat cover?

    BODI’s seat cover is closed with both zippers and Velcro enclosures to allow for easy removal of the fabric covers for cleaning purposes.
