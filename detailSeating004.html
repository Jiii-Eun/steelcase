Amia Adjustability Video
  • Does Amia have lumbar support?

    Amia is always equipped with its LiveLumbar™ System to help provide more lumbar support and comfort than comparable office chairs, made possible with height-adjustable spring force in the lumbar region that actively supports the lower back.

    Amia Air features Air LiveBack® technology, an innovative geometric pattern that flexes in two dimensions to provide responsive, personalized comfort and fit. Additional height-adjustable lumbar support is offered on Amia Air chairs that include either 3D microknit or upholstery on the back.

  • What makes Amia an ergonomic office chair?

    Amia offers several points of ergonomic support, allowing users to sit comfortably for longer periods of time.

    In addition to the standard back support on Amia (LiveLumbar™ System) and Amia Air (Air LiveBack® technology), the back tension manually adjusts to support your desired posture and provides a customized fit.

    Amia’s seat is height- and depth-adjustable, with choice of ergonomic armrests offering four-dimensional adjustability: height, width, depth, and pivot..

  • How can I adjust the Amia office chair?

    Amia and Amia Air provide several points of adjustment all over the chair to support your natural sitting style. Adjustment controls are labeled for ease of use. Here are some tips:

    - Push your hips as far back as they can go in the chair seat, so shoulders are parallel to your sit bones.

    - Adjust the seat height so your feet are flat on the floor and your knees equal to, or slightly lower than, your hips. To raise the chair, lift your body weight and pull the lever up. To lower the chair, stay seated and pull the lever up

    - Adjust the seat depth: Lift weight out of the seat when adjusting depth and pull handle and hold, slide seat forward or back, and release lever handle lock. With your back against the chair, you should have 2"- 3" of space between the back of your knees and the seat edge.

    - Adjust lumbar: Grab both handles and slide up or down. Position the lumbar to fit comfortably into the curve of your lower back

    - Adjust the armrests so that your arms are at a 90-degree angle with your hands comfortably resting on the worksurface. Amia / Amia Air feature optional height, width, depth, and pivot- adjustable arms.

    - Adjust the back tension to your desired sitting posture.

    - Upright back lock - To lock backrest in upright position, lean forward and flip lever down. Flip lever up to unlock for full-range motion.

    - Full recline range with back tension adjustment -Turn the knob forward for more resistance and backward for less.

    To learn more about how to adjust your chair, please check out the Amia and Amia Air User Guides.

  • How do I clean the Amia chair?

    The Amia chair upholstery can be cleaned with a simple mixture of 1 part clear dish soap and 16 parts water in a spray bottle, the spray should coat the surface but not soak through.

    Let sit 1-2 minutes and dry with a wet/dry vac to remove all moisture from the surface. Avoid using disinfectants, as they can damage the fabric. For more information, learn How To Clean Steelcase Products here.

  • What are Gesture’s arms made out of?

    The fully adjustable armrests on each Gesture are made out of the same soft foam-rubber material our standard armrest caps are made from, atop a durable aluminum base.

  • How do I clean the Gesture chair?

    The fabric on Gesture can be cleaned similarly to other Steelcase products, by using a mixture of one part clear dishwashing liquid and 16 parts water in a spray bottle.

    Completely coat the fabric and allow the liquid to soak in before drying it off with a wet/dry vac.

    For more information, learn How To Clean Steelcase Products here.

  • What is high performance seating?

    “High performance seating” refers to a type of office chair that is designed to ergonomically support your entire body.

    These chairs offer designs backed by research and innovation, in order to support your entire body during the workday.

    From improved posture and vascular function to reduced impact on pressure points and joints, Steelcase high performance chairs can help you do your best work with better design, better comfort, and better choice.

  • What chair is best for working long hours?

    High performance seating from Steelcase is designed to support your posture in all ways, no matter how long your workday might go.

    By encouraging a more healthy sitting position, and helping to shift with you to support your posture throughout the day, high performance seating is an ideal choice for any length of time you need to sit during the workday.

  • Who needs an ergonomic chair?

    Ergonomic chairs are encouraged for anyone who spends a substantial amount of their day seated.

    From office workers, to students or educational workers, or even to healthcare providers, high performance ergonomic seating can provide a number of benefits for anyone who sits down to work for long periods of their day.
