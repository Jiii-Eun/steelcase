  • What is Steelcase - Ready to Ship?

    Steelcase - Ready to Ship helps you get your Leap chair even more quickly than before. Ready to Ship offers a number of feature packages & configurations for your Leap chair, such as color and wheel options, allowing you to receive a preconfigured Leap in less time than it would take to assemble a custom-designed chair. The preconfigured options combine some of our most popular color choices with the advanced ergonomics and adjustability you’ve come to expect from Leap.

  • Is there a difference between Leap and Leap Quick Ship?

    Each of our Leap chairs is built with the same design and ergonomic support. The only difference for Leap - Ready to Ship is the ability to select from a range of preassembled options, allowing your chair to be assembled and shipped much more quickly than a standard Leap (within one business day in most cases).

  • Are there other color options for the Leap office chair?

    When you order a Leap - Ready to Ship, each chair is available in one of four color combinations: black fabric on black frame, graphite fabric on black frame, black fabric on platinum frame, and graphite fabric on platinum frame.

    Each of these colors are available with our preconfigured options to help get you your new Leap chair more quickly.

    If you want different color options, you can design and build your own Leap chair on the Leap Chair product page.

    For more information on the Leap chair, please visit the Leap product page.
