  • What is the maximum height of Ology?

    The maximum height of Ology is 48.7” for the extended range Ology and 46.4” for the basic range Ology. Ology is infinitely adjustable at any point before its maximum height to create a personalized sitting posture for all users.

  • How do you adjust the height of Steelcase Ology?

    Steelcase Ology comes with one of two controllers: the Simple Touch and Active Touch controllers. The Simple Touch controller can be adjusted by either lifting up on the controller to raise the desk, or pressing down on the controller to lower the desk. The Active Touch controller offers these same adjustments, but with the added ability to save predetermined heights to allow you to adjust it to your chosen height. The display will show the current height of the desk; to switch to a stored height in memory, lift or press down on the controller, the desk will pause at the stored preset. Release the controller to stop at the stored height. To reset Ology to a sitting position, simply press down on the Simple Touch controller until it returns to the desired position. If your Ology has the Active Touch controller, you can use it to return your Ology to a pre-set sitting height.

    For more information, view the Ology User Guide or our Active Touch Instructional Video.

  • Does Steelcase Divisio work with Steelcase Ology?

    Steelcase Divisio is compatible with Steelcase Ology. 21 1/2" of the screen sits on the worksurface. The depth allows the side screen to work on any 24"D or deeper worksurface.

    Diviso’s Clamp can accommodate 34"- to 112"-thick worksurfaces.
