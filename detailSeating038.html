Product Details

- The flexible seat keeps you comfortable in a variety of postures and swivels to keep sightlines open

- 5-star base allows for height adjustability and ease of mobility

- Open seat design offers easy access, while arms serve as backpack hooks

- In high-density environments, people are often forced to move against the wall and Node mid-back supports active learning, while minimizing contact

- Comfort without upholstery means easy maintenance

- Optional personal worksurface accomodates both left and right handed users and swivels in tandem with seat and is adjustable for users of all sizes

- Durable and tough enough to support up to 300 lbs.
