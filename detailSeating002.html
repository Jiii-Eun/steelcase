Product Details

Steelcase Series 1 offers an exceptional entry point into high-performance seating, characterized by space efficiency and weight-activated tension adjustments - customized to fit you.

If you’re looking for essential ergonomics at a great value, you can’t beat this chair.

- Thoughtfully designed with integrated LiveBack® flexor system that conforms to your spine to provide flexibility and support

- Comes standard with an additional adjustable lumbar for extra support

- A weight-activated mechanism takes the guesswork out of sitting and automatically responds to your body weight when you sit to provide the right amount of back tension as you recline. Allowing anyone, anywhere to sit down and get comfortable - quickly

- Not everyone is built the same, so we’ve added comfort dial with a “boost” tension setting, offering additional resistance for individuals who need it most and an upright lock

- Steelcase Series 1 doesn’t skimp on style choices either with plenty of back, seat and frame colors

We believe in doing better for the planet by recognizing our role in climate change and reducing our impact on the environment. The responsibility to do better for the world we share has always been and will always be part of who we are.

Steelcase was one of the first in the industry to achieve carbon neutrality in 2020 - and we are on track to reduce our absolute emissions 50% by 2030 - a bold goal verified by the Science Based Targets initiative.

Series 1 is where serious support, lighthearted looks and exceptional value all come together—a different class that’s still best-in-class.

- Quality & durability - Steelcase Series 1 is tested above industry standards to ensure reliability now and in the future.

- Warrantied to last - 12-year, multi-shift, on both parts and labor.
