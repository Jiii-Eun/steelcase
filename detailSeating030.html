  • What material is SILQ made from?

    SILQ’s base and frame are made from a unique polymer that bears many similarities to carbon fiber. This polymer is shaped by a Steelcase-designed process, similar to injection molding, allowing each chair to be constructed more quickly without sacrificing quality or durability.

    Additionally, the upholstery on SILQ is available in both durable fabric or premium leather.

  • How do you adjust SILQ?

    SILQ offers a unique, responsive adjustment system that automatically reacts to the way you sit. The only manual adjustment available on SILQ is its height, which is controlled by a lever on the underside of the chair. The unique material used to create SILQ is designed to respond to the natural motion of the human body as you change postures and positions throughout the day, removing the need for you to manually readjust your chair as your sitting needs change
