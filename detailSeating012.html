The Think chair was designed in response to the growing trend of mobile workers and their unique needs. Its award-winning design re-imagined what an office chair could do, the type of spaces it could be used in and how sustainable it could be. Our first office chair designed with a weight-activated mechanism – Think does the thinking for you.

- The integrated LiveBack® Flexor System conforms to your back, flexing as you change postures, preventing stiffness and soreness that can happen with less intelligent work chairs

- A weight-activated mechanism allows anyone, anywhere to sit down and get comfortable quickly by responding to your bodyweight and providing the right amount of back tension as you recline

- Not everyone is built the same, so we’ve added a comfort dial with “boost” tension setting offering additional resistance for individuals who need it most, a mid-back stop, and an upright lock

- 98% recyclable; Think is as good to the environment as it is to your body

  • How can you clean the seat on your Think chair?

    In order to keep the seat on your Think clean and disinfected without damaging the fabric, we recommend using a solution of one part clear dish detergent and 16 parts water, mixed in a spray bottle. Any harsher cleaning products than this run the risk of damaging the fabric and/or the materials used for the frame and base of the chair.

    Spray the surface of your chair evenly until damp, let it sit for 1-2 minutes, and use a wet/dry vac to remove the rest of the moisture before letting it air dry. (A similar process can be used for other Steelcase products as needed.)

  • How can you adjust a Steelcase Think?

    Think has several points of adjustment available for sitters of all heights and postures, allowing them to sit more naturally and in a way that encourages overall health and comfort. The arms are adjusted by an optional mechanism mounted on either arm that can adjust height, width, pivot, and depth. Lumbar is adjusted by two handles on the side of the chair’s back, while the seat height is adjusted via a lever on the underside of the chair.

    Angle of reclination is adjusted via our Comfort Dial near the bottom of the seat. The flexible seat edge adjusts automatically as you change positions through the day.

  • What is the difference between height adjustable arms and 4-way adjustable arms?

    Think offers two types of adjustable arms, depending on what options you select. While height adjustment comes standard on all Think chairs, allowing you to raise or lower the height of your armrests as needed, we also offer our innovative four-way adjustable arms on Think. The four-way adjustment allows you to change the height, width, pivot, and depth of each armest individually for maximum comfort no matter how your posture changes during the day.

  • How tall is Steelcase Think?

    Think has an overall height of 37.25” to 42” (adjustable), with an adjustable seat height of 16.5” to 21.5”, making it ideal for a range of preferred heights and postures.

  • Where are Steelcase Think chairs made?

    Think is manufactured by Steelcase in Kentwood, MI.
