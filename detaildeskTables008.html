Product Details

The Steelcase Flex Height-Adjustable Desk is designed to move as quickly as the day’s activities. Designed for mobility and function, the height adjustable desk comes with integrated rollers that allow for movement and one-cord-out integrated power that hosts up to seven plugs. The height-adjustable desk moves with you and your work, perfect for any home office with performance and wellbeing in mind.

If sitting at home has you down, Flex is equipped with Active Motion to remind you to move. Set personal posture reminders at your preferred intervals and track how often you sit and stand throughout the day and view progress towards your movement goals over days, weeks or months.

- Features two height range options: Extended Range, meets BIFMA G1 Ergonomic guidelines and accommodates the widest range of users – and Basic Range

- Obstruction Sensing Technology detects collision by automatically backtracking when the worksurface makes contact with an object

- Choice of an up/down Simple Touch controller or Active Touch controller with OLED display

- Visually integrated directional rollers provide a stable yet mobile work experience

- One-cord-out integrated power allows you to plug in up to five devices under the worksurface and two into the top for personal items

- Optional cable tray to keep cords hidden

- 360 lbs weight capacity

- Commercial grade high-pressure laminate (HPL) provides the strength needed to withstand wear and tear

- 12-year warranty on all mechanical, worksurface, and electronic component
