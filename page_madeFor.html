Unleash Your Potential

Soft + Residential

A fan favorite, Billiard is a woven-polyester fabric featuring a soft, felt-like texture, making it our top-pick for residential design. Billiard’s fabric contains a subtle heathered undertone allowing it to complement and soften most spaces. If you’re looking for a cozy fabric, this is it! Tested to 60,000 double rubs without pilling or changes in fabric, Billiard is built to last for years to come. Billiard is made from 100% polyester with antimony-free yarn, making it safe for you and those in your home.

Contemporary + Refined

Meet Era, a soft fabric with a subtle herringbone texture that creates a refined, sophisticated and contemporary look. The versatility of this fabric makes it a perfect complement to any home workspace. Wear tested to withstand high-traffic situations - kids, pets and daily use are no match for Era. For long-lasting durability, Era has been wear-tested to 100,000 double rubs. Made from 100% polyester with antimony-free yarn, Era's OEKO-TEX® Standard 100 certification ensures every component of the product, from the fabric to the thread, has been rigorously tested against OEKO-TEX®'s Standard list of 350+ toxic chemicals - something we know both, you and mother nature, will appreciate.

Breathable + Dynamic

Intermix, our proprietary performance fabric, creates perfect tension on the seat and back frame for the ideal ergonomic support. It never sags or feels stiff and provides the perfect amount of breathability for your long work (or gaming) sessions.  Intermix comes in three distinct weaves, each with its own unique look. Intermix: a translucent fabric with a breathable open weave. Intermix Opaque: our breathable Intermix weave combined with an upholstered look and soft-hand feel. Intermix Shift: a translucent fabrics with a breathable open weave that subtly changes color at different angles and in different lighting.  Intermix and its three distinct weaves are made from 70% polyester and 30% nylon and with antimony-free yarn. Intermix is wear-tested to 60,000 double rubs to ensure long-lasting durability and comfort, coupled with a sleek design for your home.

Renewable + Vibrant

Meet Cogent: Connect, a knit-like material that is exceedingly durable and comes in a wide range of color options. This textile is well-suited for high-traffic environments and daily use. Made in the U.S.A, this durable fabric is wear-tested to 100,000 double rubs. Cogent: Connect is made from 100% polyester with antimony-free yarn. Cogent: Connect is Cradle-to-Cradle certified making it safe for you and those in your home. Crafted with a minimum of 50% renewable energy, Cogent: Connect is the perfect complement to support your efforts to reduce your impact on the environment. 

Luxurious + Smooth

Bring a luxurious feeling to any workspace with our Elmosoft leather. This soft and pliable leather is made with semi-aniline dye that smooths the natural grain of the leather for a uniform appearance. With a curated color range, from soft neutrals to muted tones, it combines outstanding comfort with high durability for an office chair that looks as great as it feels. Elmosoft leather is crafted to not patina with time. Wear-tested for durability, exposure to wet and dry conditions, and light, our leather will maintain its beautiful color and integrity for years to come. Made from 100% leather, Steelcase Elmosoft leather is crafted with sustainable and clean water tanning technology that allows water to be borrowed and returned to its source in its original condition. A by-product of the food industry, Steelcase Elmosoft leather diverts hides from entering the landfill and is finished without use of solvents.

FAQs

  • Steelcase materials are contract-grade. What does that mean? 

    Steelcase fabrics meet commercial-grade standards, which are significantly higher than those required for normal household use. This means your chair can be both aesthetically pleasing and handle constant use without showing signs of wear.

  • What is a double rub count? Why is it important?

    All of our fabrics are tested for two types of crocking: wet (color transfer from wet fabric) and dry (color transfer from dry fabric). All Steelcase fabrics meet or exceed contract-industry standards, meaning you never have to worry about your chair transferring color onto your clothes or other surfaces.

  • I'd love a bright bold color, but I am worried about the color transferring. Will my chair's color transfer?

    All of our fabrics are tested for two types of crocking: wet (color transfer from wet fabric) and dry (color transfer from dry fabric). All Steelcase fabrics meet or exceed contract-industry standards, meaning you never have to worry about your chair transferring color onto your clothes or other surfaces. 

  • I plan to position my chair near a window, will the fabric fade over time?

    When exposed to UV light, all colors fade over time, however, Steelcase fabrics are put through rigorous UV testing to ensure their ability to stand up to colorfastness is exceptional. All of our fabrics meet or exceed contract-industry standards for colorfastness to UV light. And for what it's worth, we position many of our own chairs (both in our corporate and home offices) near windows because we are confident in our fabrics' ability to withstand UV light for long periods.

  • Oops, I spilled on my chair. How can I clean my Steelcase fabric?

    All Steelcase fabrics have a "WS" cleaning code, except our Elmosoft leather which has a "W" cleaning code.

    For removing water-based marks or stains (such as coffee, milk, soft drinks, fruit juices, washable ink, etc.):

    - First, gently apply a water-based cleaner, that is specifically made for cleaning fabric, to the soiled area using a clean cloth or a sponge.

    - Second, if possible, work the cleaner into a lather to minimize soaking the fabric. Using light brushing motions, brush from the outside of the soiled area towards the center to prevent rings.

    - Finally, allow the fabric to dry completely, then vacuum thoroughly.

    For removing oil-based marks or stains (such as salad dressing, grease, lipstick, ballpoint ink, etc.):

    - First, gently apply a dry-cleaning fluid to the soiled area using a dampened, clean cloth or soft-bristle brush.

    - Next, using quick, light, rubbing or brushing strokes, work from the outside of the soiled area toward the center to prevent rings. Avoid soaking the fabric.

    - Then, gently blot with a clean, absorbent cloth to soak up and remove dampened soil.

    - Finally, allow the fabric to dry completely, then vacuum thoroughly.

  • Oops, I spilled on my chair. How can I clean my Steelcase leather?

    Dusting with a clean, dry cloth is the recommended routine cleaning method for all Steelcase semi-aniline-dyed, full-grain leather.

    For removing spots or spills:

    - First, wipe away excess liquid immediately and let air dry.

    - Second, if necessary, work lukewarm water and a mild non-detergent soap into a thin lather with a soft cloth and rinse with clean water.

    - Finally, gently buff the leather to restore luster.

    Note: The use of any other product could cause reactions with the solvent-free chemicals in the tanning process.

  • My high-performance seating chair comes with a 12-year Steelcase warranty. Does that include the chair's fabric too?

    Yes, our Steelcase 12-year warranty protects your chair's fabric against any defects or workmanship issues. Like many warranties, ours does not cover general wear and tear, but rest assured, Steelcase fabrics are tested to the limits so we're confident they can stand up to daily life.
