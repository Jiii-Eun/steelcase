Product Details

Frequently Asked Questions

Looking to add movement to your day? Turnstone Buoy’s curved base, rocking motion and height adjustability engage your core and promote good posture.

- 18” diameter upholstered cap – available in 14 fabrics to add color to your space

- Built-in handle – used to adjust height and easier to carry

- Plastic canister – easy cleaning and maintenance of the Buoy base. For an even easier cleaning experience on the cap, choose the Merle Durable Foam Cap

- Curved base – rocks to encourage active sitting

Overall Height 17.25″ – 22.75″
Overall Width 18″
Overall Depth 18″
Shipping Box Dimensions 33″ H x 32″ W x 32″ D
Shipping Box Weight 30 lbs.
  • How does Buoy support your posture?

    Buoy’s unique design allows for continued posture changes throughout the workday, by providing an active sitting base that moves when you do. The curved base and balanced center of Buoy encourage you to sit with proper posture, helping you to strengthen your core muscles as you do.

  • What are the benefits of Buoy?

    Buoy offers a much more active sitting experience than similar office stools. As Buoy is designed without a seatback, it encourages you to sit with better posture in order to keep the stool straight and remain comfortable at your desk throughout the day. Additionally, the rounded base can tilt as you change posture or positions, helping you to exercise muscles that typically don’t get much use throughout the workday. In certain cases, this combination of posture encouragement and low-intensity workout can help people who find themselves suffering back pain from more ‘traditional’ office seating solutions.

  • What are Buoy’s dimensions?

    Buoy has a width of 18 inches, a depth of 18 inches, and an adjustable height of 17.25 inches to 22.75 inches (with a five and a half-inch adjustment range), allowing Buoy to fit in seamlessly with nearly any workspace. Buoy also weighs 20 lbs and offers a built-in handle, allowing it to be easily moved wherever you need it to be.

  • How do I clean Buoy?

    The fabric top of Buoy can be cleaned with any neutral cleaning solution, such as soapy water. A wet/dry vacuum can be used in the event of major spills, and certain common stains such as coffee, juice, or ink can be treated with soap flakes, washing liquid, or color-fixing agents as needed. The hard surface of the base shell can be cleaned with a mixture of mild soap and water, in a spray bottle or on a soft cloth as needed.

  • How do I adjust Buoy’s height?

    Buoy’s height is adjusted by the lever on the front of the seat. Depressing the lever while sitting on Buoy lowers the height, while depressing the lever without sitting on it raises the height. Buoy has a 5.5-inch adjustable range of 17.25 inches to 22.75 inches.
