Product Details

Designed by Patricia Urquiola, Hosu is a lounge chair that invites you to spread out. When it’s time to get things done, Hosu features cable pass-through at the base to allow laptops, tablets and mobile devices to easily connect, plus rear and side storage pockets. Its unique textured fabric, also designed by Urquiola, is offered in six exclusive colors.

- Side and rear pockets for light storage

- Standard with one soft lumbar pillow

- Wire pass through at the base

- Available upholstered in custom designed Hexa fabric

Overall Height 35″
Overall Width 34.6″
Overall Depth 35.6″
Seat Height from Floor 14″
