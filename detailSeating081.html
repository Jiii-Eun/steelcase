Product Details

Enea Altzo943 brings residential warmth and soul with elevated design and elegant detail that complement many styles. Enea Altzo943 combines the clean curves of a precision-molded seat shell with the sculptural refinement of solid oak legs.

Enjoy more freedom of expression with mixed material and color combinations, to make social seating that’s as alluring in style as it is in comfort.

- Polypropylene seat shell

- Metal seat casting painted to match shell

- Solid oak wood leg with polyurethane glide

- Optional half inch thick upholstered insert

- Upholstered insert stitch detail

Overall Height 30.5″
Overall Width 20.75″
Overall Depth 20.75″
Seat Height 18″
