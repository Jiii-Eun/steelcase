Payment + Financing

  • It is our goal to make purchasing a new Steelcase office chair, desk or accessory as easy as possible. We currently accept Visa, Mastercard and American Express. You can also pay through your PayPal account. In addition, we offer convenient financing through PayPal Credit—allowing you to buy now and pay overtime.

  • If you are shopping from Canada, your browser should automatically detect your location and update pricing to CAD. If you would like to manually change to CAD, you may do so by clicking the globe in the top left corner of the navigation and selecting “ENGLISH (CAN)” as your region.

  • Credit and Debit Cards must be associated with a billing address within the United States and Canada.

    Steelcase obtains a pre-approval from the card company for the order, which may result in a corresponding block on your available credit while the pre-approval remains in place. Debit cards and check cards may have daily spending limits that may delay order processing. Steelcase will not bill your credit card or process a transaction under your debit or check card until your order ships. If your order contains multiple items and they ship at different times, you may see multiple charges on your credit card statement.

  • We offer PayPal Credit as an optional payment method. PayPal Credit allows you to buy now and make payments over time. Paying with PayPal keeps all your financial information stored in your PayPal account. It is not shared with the Steelcase Store. If you pay with PayPal, at checkout, you will be asked to:

    - Log into your PayPal account.

    - Confirm the information on the PayPal web page.

    - Submit your payment.

    You will then be automatically directed back to the Steelcase Store to review your order. For more information on PayPal Credit, learn more Here.

  • Upon purchase through the Steelcase Store, your credit card will reflect an authorization charge—these often show up as pending charges on your credit card. These authorizations will appear on your account until they expire based on the issuing bank or your order has shipped in full. If the authorization expires before the product ships, you may see a reauthorization. When your product(s) have shipped this authorization will be replaced by the final charge. If your order contains multiple items and they ship at different times, you may see a new authorization amount appear for the remaining balance of the order after we ship any part of the order. If the order is cancelled, this authorization charge will fall off in 1-3 day(s).

  • If your company has a Work from Home Employee Discount Program with Steelcase, you must register on the site to access your discount.

    Click on the Log In/Register icon in the upper right-hand corner and choose the Register option to create a new account. Be sure to use your company email when creating your account.
