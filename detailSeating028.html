Product Details

A fresh take on the Amia stool, Amia Air offers the same adjustments, seat and arms as Amia with a specifically engineered Air Back design that offers a more transparent and airy aesthetic

- Air LiveBack® technology works through an innovative geometric pattern in the polypropylene back that flexes in two dimensions to encourage movement and support posture changes

- Custom manual adjustment controls allow the user to tailor in their perfect fit. The adjustment controls are labeled, eliminating any question about how to dial-in the perfect fit for your perfect sit

- The seat includes flexible edges for a pressure-free sit and adaptive bolstering in the seat cushion – which allows the seat to conform to the body distributing the user’s weight to allow for a comfortable sit for any size user.

- Perfect as you work at counter and bar-height tables and move frequently from sitting to standing

Ideal for group spaces and conference rooms where you can be engaged, yet comfortable for shorter periods of time

All Steelcase High Performance task seating is contract grade quality:

- Quality & durability – Amia Air is tested above industry standards to ensure reliability now and in the future.

- Warrantied to last – 12-year, multi-shift, on both parts and labor.

- Free shipping + returns – Free FedEx delivery and free returns.
