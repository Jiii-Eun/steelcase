  • How does the Universal Cable Management Kit help me manage cables in the office?

    The Steelcase Universal Cable Management Kit offers a unique way to manage cable clutter underneath your desk. Its unique, basket-like design offers a tray that can hide away your cables and feed them to their destination more smoothly, while the suitcase-inspired smart straps inside help keep your cables untangled and flowing.

  • Will the Universal Cable Management Kit work with my desk?

    Yes! The Universal Cable Management Kit is designed to attach under the desktop (using screws) of nearly any modern office desk or work surface, and is available in two different sizes to accommodate a wide range of cable quantities.

  • How do I access my cables inside the tray?

    A hinged design on the back of the tray (facing away from you when sitting at your desk) allows you to easily insert your cables, and the open design on the front allows you to organize, unplug, or access them as needed.

  • How do I set up the Universal Cable Management Kit?

    The Universal Cable Management Kit is installed underneath your existing desk by clamping it to the desired location temporarily, and drilling through the appropriate holes on the topmost brackets of the tray. From there, cables should be organized while the tray is in the open position, then cords can be threaded through the top or bottom openings. and separate them with the included straps before plugging them back into their needed devices.

    For more in depth assembly instructions, refer to our Universal Cable Management Kit Assembly Guide.

  • Can the Universal Cable Management Kit work with a standing desk?

    Yes! The Universal Cable Management Kit comes with two differently sized trays to help it fit underneath a wide array of desks, including standing desks. As long as your desk has sufficient space to mount the kit underneath, it will be compatible!
