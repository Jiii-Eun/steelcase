Product Details

Voted best office chair*, Gesture offers best-in-class features and performance. Likewise, the Stool version of our best-selling chair supports a large range of postures and users with superior technology.

- Designed with 3D LiveBack®, the contoured backrest aligns perfectly with your spine’s natural S-shape, providing continuous lower back support

- Perfect as you work at counter and bar-height tables and move frequently from sitting to standing

- Ideal for group spaces and conference rooms where you can be engaged, yet comfortable for shorter periods of time

- A flexible seat edge in the front relieves pressure points under your thighs while the soft edge around the perimeter supports different sitting style

- Fully adjustable 360 degree arms, mimic the human arm for comfortable support as you work on multiple tasks. As you move, the armrests remain parallel to the worksurface as you move, so you can stay comfortable and connected at every angle of recline

- You can dial in the Gesture stool for a more precise fit. All adjustments are located on the right-hand side within an arm’s reach—easy to find and use

If you’re looking for the ultimate flexibility, comfort and support in a stool, as you connect with your team or sit at a drafting table, Gesture is the stool for you.
